Programmes

As part of our efforts to promote improved self-care among people with diabetes,
we strive towards effective, convenient and reasonably priced programmes for
education, counseling and support as well as complication screening for our members.

Our services are provided at low cost with support from well-wishers and through our fund-raising activities.
